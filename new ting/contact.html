Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on MetaDate? +

Creating an account is easy! Just click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right of any page, fill in your details, and follow the simple verification process. You'll be ready to start matching in minutes.

Is MetaDate free to use? +

Yes, MetaDate offers a free basic membership that allows you to create a profile, browse other users, and send a limited number of messages. We also offer premium subscriptions with additional features for those who want to enhance their experience.

How does the matching algorithm work? +

Our algorithm considers multiple factors including your preferences, interests, values, and behavior on the platform. The more complete your profile and the more you interact with the platform, the better our suggestions will be.

How can I report inappropriate behavior? +

You can report any user by visiting their profile and clicking the "Report" button. Our moderation team reviews all reports promptly and takes appropriate action to maintain a safe community.

Can I use MetaDate to find friends or business connections? +

Absolutely! While MetaDate is primarily a dating platform, we have specific categories for friendship and business networking. Just select your preferred category when setting up your profile.
