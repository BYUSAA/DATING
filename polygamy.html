The Ethical Polygamy Guide

How to navigate consensual non-monogamy with respect.

Communication

+

Open, honest dialogue is the foundation of ethical polygamy. Discuss:

  • Boundaries and expectations
  • Emotional needs and check-ins
  • Safe sex practices

Jealousy Management

+

Healthy ways to process jealousy:

  • Identify root causes (fear, insecurity)
  • Use "I feel" statements
  • Practice compersion (joy in your partner's joy)

Legal Considerations

+

Understand the legal landscape:

  • Marriage laws in your region
  • Cohabitation agreements
  • Parenting rights
