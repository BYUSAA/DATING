About MetaDate

Our mission to connect souls across dimensions

Our Story

MetaDate was founded in 2023 with a vision to revolutionize the way people connect in the digital age. We believe that meaningful relationships can transcend physical boundaries when powered by cutting-edge technology and genuine human values.

Our Technology

Using advanced AI algorithms and neural matching systems, MetaDate analyzes thousands of data points to connect you with the most compatible individuals based on your personality, values, and relationship goals.

Our Team

We're a diverse team of relationship experts, data scientists, and technologists passionate about creating connections that matter. Our headquarters are in San Francisco, with team members spanning across 12 countries.
