OUR STORY

Founded in 2023, Metadatez began as a vision to revolutionize how people connect in the digital age. Our team of relationship experts, technologists, and futurists came together with one mission: to create a platform that transcends traditional dating boundaries.

We recognized that human connections are multi-dimensional, yet most platforms force people into narrow categories. Metadatez was born to change that, offering a space where all types of relationships can flourish.

From our cutting-edge AI matching system to our innovative credit-based interaction model, every aspect of Metadatez is designed with the future of human connection in mind.
