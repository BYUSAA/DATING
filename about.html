Who We Are

MetaDate was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to create meaningful connections in a digital world. What began as a small startup has grown into a global platform helping millions find love, friendship, and everything in between.

Our team of relationship experts, technologists, and designers work tirelessly to create an experience that feels both modern and human. We believe technology should enhance relationships, not replace the magic of real connection.

Unlike other platforms, we focus on quality over quantity, using smart matching algorithms and verified profiles to create safer, more meaningful interactions.
