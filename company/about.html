About Metadatez

Redefining connections in the digital age

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Metadatez was born from a simple idea: human connections should be as diverse as humanity itself. We noticed that existing platforms forced people into narrow categories, limiting their ability to find the exact type of connection they were looking for.

Our team of relationship experts, technologists, and designers came together to create a platform that celebrates all forms of human connection - from romantic relationships to friendships, professional networking to inmate correspondence.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, inclusive platform where people can find exactly the type of connection they're looking for, with the tools and technology to make those connections meaningful and lasting.
