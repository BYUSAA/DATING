FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum). All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel during a billing period, you'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of that billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

We occasionally offer free trials for new members. Check our promotions page or sign up for our newsletter to be notified about upcoming trial offers.

How do I change my subscription plan?

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Are there discounts for long-term commitments?

Yes! Our yearly plans offer significant savings (up to 20%) compared to paying monthly. We also occasionally offer special promotions for longer commitments.

What happens if I don't renew my subscription?

If you choose not to renew, your account will revert to our free plan. You won't lose any data, but access to premium features will be restricted.
