FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website and follow the instructions to create your profile.

Is Metadatez available in my country?

Metadatez is currently available worldwide, though some features may be limited in certain regions due to local laws.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account at any time in your account settings under the "Privacy" section.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and various cryptocurrencies for subscription payments.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

You can report any user or content by clicking the "Report" button on their profile or in the message interface.

Can I get a refund for my subscription?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. After that period, refunds are not typically provided.
