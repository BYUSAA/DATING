Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about MetaDate

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password. If you don't see the email, please check your spam folder.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data and never share your personal information with third parties without your consent. You can review our Privacy Policy for more details.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our algorithm considers your profile information, preferences, and behavior on the platform to suggest compatible matches. The more you use MetaDate and interact with profiles, the better our suggestions become.

Can I pause my subscription?

Yes, you can pause your premium subscription at any time from your account settings. During the pause period, you won't be charged but will retain access until your current billing period ends.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

You can report any profile or message by clicking the "Report" button (flag icon) on their profile or in the conversation. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours and takes appropriate action.
