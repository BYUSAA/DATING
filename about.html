Who We Are

MetaDate was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to help people find meaningful connections in an increasingly digital world. Our team of relationship experts, technologists, and designers came together to create a platform that goes beyond superficial swiping.

We believe that everyone deserves to find love, friendship, or companionship, regardless of their background, preferences, or circumstances. Our advanced matching algorithms and diverse community make it easier than ever to find your perfect match.
