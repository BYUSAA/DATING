Our Story

MetaDate was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to create a dating platform that goes beyond superficial connections. We believe that technology should enhance human relationships, not replace them.

Our team of relationship experts, data scientists, and engineers have worked tirelessly to develop an algorithm that considers not just your preferences, but your values, lifestyle, and long-term goals.

Our Vision

We envision a world where finding meaningful connections is effortless and enjoyable. Whether you're looking for casual dating, marriage, friendship, or something in between, MetaDate provides a safe, inclusive space to explore relationships.

Our platform is designed to adapt to your needs, with specialized categories that cater to different relationship goals and lifestyles.
