FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes will take effect immediately, and you'll only be charged the prorated difference if upgrading.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime with no penalty.

How does the yearly billing work?

When you choose yearly billing, you'll be charged for the full year upfront at a 20% discount. Your subscription will automatically renew each year unless canceled.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and various cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin and Ethereum.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. After that period, we don't offer refunds but you can cancel anytime to stop future billing.
