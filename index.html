About MetaDate

Our mission to connect souls across dimensions

Our Story

MetaDate was founded in 2023 with a vision to revolutionize the way people connect in the digital age. We believe that meaningful relationships can transcend physical boundaries when powered by cutting-edge technology and genuine human values.

Our Technology

Using advanced AI algorithms and neural matching systems, MetaDate analyzes thousands of data points to connect you with the most compatible individuals based on your personality, values, and relationship goals.

Our Team

We're a diverse team of relationship experts, data scientists, and technologists passionate about creating connections that matter. Our headquarters are in San Francisco, with team members spanning across 12 countries.

Privacy Policy

How we protect and use your information

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, including your name, email address, age, gender, and preferences. We also collect data about your interactions with our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, personalize your experience, facilitate matches, and communicate with you. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also opt out of marketing communications at any time.
